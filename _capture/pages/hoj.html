BRANDING
An identity that finds joy in the overlooked.
House of Joy is a pattern studio built on the belief that fulfilment is personal—not found in status or validation. Our patterns act as emotional touchpoints, triggering memory and reflection. Choosing them is a quiet form of self-care, turning your space into a daily reminder of who you are and what matters to you. In a world that rushes us forward, we invite you to pause, reconnect, and notice what’s been there all along. Because joy is hidden in plain sight—waiting to be seen. House of Joy is a realisation, a celebration of the overlooked.
