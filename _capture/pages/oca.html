BRANDING
What's the deal with teal?
OCA is a charity to help support those going through the difficulties with Ovarian Cancer.

Our rebrand focuses on looking at the 2 ovaries- a swollen ovary signifing the presence of ovarian cancer. The use of teal has been chosen as this is the colour for ovarian cancer. The logo symbolises the cancer ribbon. We have created a brand that not only focuses on the woman going through ovarian cancer, but also the many others affected such as their close friends and family.
