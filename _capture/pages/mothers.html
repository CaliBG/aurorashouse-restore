Mothers
TYPE
A bespoke typeface for a nostalgic series.
A bespoke typeface created to establish a brand image for ‘Mothers’ pitchdeck - a new TV series pitched for Apple TV written about a rock club set in Birmingham in the late 1960’s. The typeface was originally hand drawn before being refined digitally.
