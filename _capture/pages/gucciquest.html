A pitch for increasing customer engagement.
Gucci Quest is a NFT based treasure hunt, involving Gucci releasing clues for their customers to solve and win prizes. The pitch deck was designed to explain how the Quest would work, its benefits for Gucci as well as visualising an aesthetic for the Quest.
