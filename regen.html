ReGen
BRANDING
ReGen: Energy for a fairer future.
Regen’s rebrand reflects their mission to create a fairer, more sustainable world that moves us forward. As a renewable energy consultancy, Regen values collaboration, progress, and listening to communities and businesses. Bold, interlocking patterns represent individuals and networks working together, symbolizing connection and growth. Paired with modern sans-serif and warm serif typefaces, the design balances technological innovation with a human-centered approach.
