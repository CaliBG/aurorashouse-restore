About me
Jellyfied
BRANDING
How can we encourage society to become more open about hard questions?
An app for the socially curious. Jellyfied is a social platform designed for users to share thoughts on unanswered topics and debates. The goal of Jellyfied is for users to discuss polarizing topics in hopes to get to a common ground. Jellyfied gets you to start a conversation with questions and encourage other users to participate in the discussion.
