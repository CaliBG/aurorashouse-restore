Kiri
BRANDING
From pixel to pattern
Kiri is a minimalist typeface driven by a passion for grids and geometric order. Built on a strict, unified system, the design encompasses all 26 letters and 10 numerals. Its defining feature is its dual nature: at small scales, it functions as a crisp, legible pixel font, but when scaled up, it reveals intricate decorative details. To demonstrate its versatility, Kiri was applied to greeting cards as custom monograms, showcasing how a rigid mathematical system can still express unique personal identity.
