About me
Giving a warm identity to a corporate industry.
Rossco is a London-based property firm specialising in providing an active network for their clients to build connections. Our rebranding focused on Rossco's personal client relationships, symbolised by the 'Rossco rings'. One ring resembles Rossco’s journey and the other ring resembles the clients journey- meeting at an interlock.

We gave Rossco their tagline ‘Always being there when it matters’ to show their supportive nature and their goal to build lasting relationships.
