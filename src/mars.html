Mars
CAMPAIGN
A campaign to launch Mars' new sustainable initiative
Mars — the makers of M&M's, Snickers, Ben's Originals, Twix ad more needed a campaign to showcase their sustainability programme. We created a media campaign, working across many platforms, using video and print to delievers Mars's new 'Healthy Planet Iniative'.

The Campaign was delivered across the UK and US, having dramatically succeeded Mars' expected impressions.
