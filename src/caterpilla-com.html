Caterpilla
BRANDING
A space for creatives to get feedback from other creatives on their work.
Caterpilla is a social platform for creatives to share their work, and build their community. The platform allows users to share their recent work for other users and industry professionals to provide feedback. The platform is built to support both emerging and established creatives, providing a space to refine their craft and expand their network.
